Sycotalk

Mobile App Design

Sycotalk was formed initially due to the lack of awareness of metal health in Indonesia. By providing affordable online consultation media, it is hoped that it will reduce the number of deaths caused by suicide. The Covid-19 pandemic has affected various aspects of people's lives, especially in terms of health. Not only physical health, mental health or mental health also needs to be considered. Many young people feel isolated from the outside world and their friends, which results in a lack of social contact which can lead to depression and end up with self-defense problems. From this problem, sycotalk is here to provide a platform for someone to share their problem with psychologists that is easy to access anywhere.
